ABOUT US

Selizec Energy Company is a local Oil marketing Company which was established in 2019 to procure and distribute petroleum products, namely Automotive Diesel Oil, Unleaded Premium, All grades of Lubricating Oils, Liquefied Petroleum Gas (LPG), Greases and other Specialties in Zambia and also export to other Countries in the region.

Selizec Energy Company entry into the energy was prompted by existing Gap in the market (opportunity) coupled with the exiting appetite in the sector to offer value.

The founders of the SECOL pride themselves with vast downstream knowledge that spans over 20 years gained from experiences from working in senior management portfolios of major Oil Marketing Companies in kenya and East African Region.

The main Selizec KPIs includes strict adherence to Quality, Environment, Health and Safety (QEHS) for all products and Services, Zero delays in responding to customer orders, queries and complaints, highest professional standards of products and Services and highest standard of training for her employees.

Selizec's vision is to be a leading petroleum products provider in Kenya and the region offering Value, Reliability, Quality and Relationship based alternatives on a Win Win basis.

OWNERSHIP

Selizec Energy Company is local company, registered and Incorporated with the registrar of companies in Kenya as a company limited by shares and trading in petroleum products and non-fuel products.

Selizec Energy Company was established in 2020 to provide Energy solutions and share the specialized and vast experience attained by its Directors and Management in petroleum products and services.
